# Sunsprout v0.1.0 — First Sprout 🌱

The very first slice. The village is built, the soil is ready, and four villagers want to meet you.

## What's in

- **40×30 tile world** — procedurally generated grass, plaza, paths, pond, well, inn, shop, farmhouse, and an 8×6 tilled starter patch
- **4 crops** — wheat, tomato, pumpkin, flower (each with 3–5 growth stages and real prices)
- **4 villagers** — Mayor Bramble, Maple the Shopkeep, Finn the Fisher, Rose the Innkeeper — each with daily schedules and rotating warm dialogue
- **Day/night cycle** with sky gradient + tint overlay
- **4 seasons of 7 days each** surfaced in the HUD
- **3 starter quests** — First Sprout, A Good Harvest, Good Neighbour
- **Gold economy** — start at 50g, buy seeds, sell-all at the village well
- **HUD** — top status bar + bottom hotbar + quest panel + toast notifications
- **Dialogue box** with NPC name, role, and wrap-to-fit text

## Known limits

- No save — your day starts fresh every page load.
- NPCs drift toward schedule anchors but don't pathfind around buildings.
- Audio is silent.
- Camera doesn't ease out at world edges (intentional — feels tight).
