## What this batch was actually about

This was the "set up the next chapter" tick. Observation #2 from
ticks #30 and #31 had been flagging for two ticks that the dawn-toast
composer was approaching its tail budget — at 7 tails the morning toast
was readable but a wall of equal-weight " · "-separated phrases. The
right move was to ship the tail-grouping refactor BEFORE the 8th
tail landed, then prove out the new shape with new tails on the same
tick. That's what happened:

1. **Tail grouping refactor (57909ea)** — `assembleDawnToast` keeps
   its existing flat-array signature for back-compat but ALSO accepts
   a new `{system?, achievement?}` grouped object. System tails get
   the regular " · " rhythm; achievement tails join with each other
   using " • " inside ONE " · "-attached cluster. The engine swaps
   the 7-tail dawn through the grouped form. Pure refactor: byte-for-
   byte identical output when the achievement bucket has ≤1 brag
   (because the bullet is a SEPARATOR, not a prefix).

2. **Chain-recipient brag (29fd938)** — first new tail since the
   refactor. Validates the grouping on the same tick it shipped.
   `OWL_CHAIN_RECIPIENT_BRAG_LENGTH=25` mirrors `OWL_FLUENT_MILESTONE`.
   Per-recipient sticky map (`chainRecipientFired[npcId]`) means each
   NPC earns the brag once but a separate 25-day chain to a different
   recipient unlocks its own brag. Drops into `achievement: [...]`
   array as a single line. Also tightened the audit on chainTierDawnBrag
   — it had been passing `null` for its firedKey because the audit
   flag didn't exist when it shipped; now passes the canonical field
   name and the audit flag flows through persistence properly.

3. **Split-record brag (d6c367e)** — second new tail. Fires once the
   morning `bestRun.countDay` and `bestRun.goldDay` first diverge.
   The pre-update `wasSplit` snapshot inside `resetMineHaul` is the
   important guard — it means saves that were already split before
   the flag existed do NOT spuriously arm. Merge-back-then-split-
   again doesn't re-fire (audit flag holds).

4. **Owl-menu chain countdown chip (f3d95be)** — passive UI chip on
   the active-chain-target row. `activeChainCountdownChip` returns
   "Nd streak" when the npcId is the active chain target AND the
   chain hasn't broken. Length-1 streaks DO get the chip — fills the
   "streak exists but no bonus yet" gap below the chain-bonus floor.
   Positioned via `ctx.measureText` so the streak chip slots next to
   the bonus chip when both fire and stands alone when only the
   streak chip applies.

5. **Vein-connoisseur achievement (9f4159a)** — 26th badge. Lifetime
   10 of EVERY gem type. Lazy-ledger via the existing `lifetimeCounts`
   map. Orthogonal to cave-veteran (lifetime-100-total): the breadth
   badge is unshortcuttable with a one-gem grind. `5 * 10 = 50` total
   sits below the 100-gem cave-veteran bar so both badges track in
   parallel — they don't lap each other.
