## Notes
- Every new placeable / item flowed cleanly through the existing
  persistence forward-compat pattern (an optional field on the save
  snapshot that defaults to empty / a fresh state on older saves).
  No SAVE_VERSION bump was needed.
- Forage and coop both extended the well-sell loop in game.ts so a
  single trip to the well sells harvest + gems + forage + eggs.
- The egg + forage recipe tier had to teach `rawSellValue()` two new
  inventory namespaces (`egg`, `forage-*`) so the markup-invariant
  test now actually checks a non-zero raw price.
- Dog state lives on the World as a single FarmDogState struct (not a
  list), persisted directly. Pet streak caps at 14 to keep the
  morale economy from snowballing on long saves.
- Greenhouse converts grass tiles to tilled on placement and is then
  rendered as a translucent glass frame over them. Bonus growth is
  capped per crop's growthStages so wheat can't blow past stage 2.
- Three keybinds landed on letters reserved for future roadmap rows
  (`Y` for achievements, `J` for crop journal, `U` for something
  TBD). Documented the conflict in STATE.md so the next tick picks
  fresh letters instead of stomping.
